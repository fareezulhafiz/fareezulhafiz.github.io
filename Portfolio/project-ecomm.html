01 // Domain & Case Study

The Smart Campus

E-Community V2 addresses the daily challenges of large university campuses where students struggle with complex layouts and fragmented information. According to 2024 MOHE data, 60% of students find navigating facilities difficult. This project replaces outdated notice boards and static maps with a centralized digital ecosystem.

Centralized platform for navigation, scheduling, and wellness.
02 // The Problem

Information Fragmentation

Students face four major hurdles: navigation confusion, unreliable academic communication, inefficient lost-and-found reporting via social media, and a lack of integrated health tracking. 45% of students report frustration with late schedule updates, which negatively impacts academic preparedness.

Bridging the gap between theory and daily campus efficiency.
03 // The Modules

QuickNav & Integrated Services

The solution features QuickNav for GPS-guided navigation and emergency SOS, a Timetable manager with automated reminders, a community-based Lost & Found system, and an Accelerometer-based Step Counter to encourage healthy campus activity.

Leveraging smartphone sensors for a modern digital solution.
