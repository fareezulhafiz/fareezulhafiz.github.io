RESPAWNED

Survival Horror: Escape the heart of a failed mutation.

Genre

Horror / Survival

Platform

PC (Windows)

Rating

18+ (ESRB)

Publisher

Taka Studios

01 // High Concept

The Survivor's Tale

Respawned is a third-person survival horror game set in a dystopian world ravaged by a failed medical experiment. Players assume the role of a lone survivor awakening in an abandoned hospital, ground zero of a zombie outbreak and must venture through decaying industrial landscapes to uncover the dark truth.

Immersive third-person survival horror atmosphere.
02 // Combat & Evolution

Evolve or Perish

The core gameplay centers on resource management and tactical evolution. Clearing levels unlocks a branching talent tree, allowing players to choose between a "Strategic" or "Barbaric" path. Survival depends on scavenging limited ammunition and healing items, as health does not regenerate automatically.

Branching talent trees and limited resource management.
03 // The Horde

Adaptive Threats

Zombies react to noise and movement, forcing players to manage their pace carefully. From camouflaged agile undead in swamps to high-threat industrial mutants, each enemy type possesses unique abilities that challenge the player's quick thinking and raw determination.

Enemy AI reacts to environmental noise and movement.
Core Objectives

Survive. Adapt. Escape.

Navigate treacherous environments, from abandoned hospitals to sinister factories, guided by a mysterious soldier to bridge the gap between theory and survival.
